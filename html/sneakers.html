Adidas NMD Hu Pharrell x Chanel

Discription :

Adidas Predator are a range of football boots developed by German sportswear manufacturer Adidas,
introduced in 1994.The Predator boots are based on a prototype concept from the Australian former footballer Craig Johnston.
A characteristic feature of the Predator range is the presence of rubber patches or strips on the top of the shoe,
designed to increase friction between the boot and the ball.
In late 2010, Adidas designed the new "Power-spine" technology,
which they claim improves shot power by reducing the amount the foot bends back as it kicks the ball.
Only 5 pairs of the Adidas NMD Hu Pharrell x Chanel were made, and they were given to specific individuals.
